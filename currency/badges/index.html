What are badges?

Warning: This page contains a lot of images and may eat through metered connections.Badges are basically achievements that you can gain. Badges will show on your profile in the order shown below. Additionally, the Badge icon of your highest badge will be drawn onto you profile picture in the embeds thumbnail.Note: Almost all to all badges are applied to you after doing /profile show so use that first and then the /badges show command.
